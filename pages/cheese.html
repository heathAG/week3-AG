Fun Facts about Cheese

  • The most popular cheese recipe in the United States is "macaroni and cheese."
  • There are around 2,000 varieties of cheese.
  • Cheese takes up about 1/10 the volume of the milk it was made from.
  • A giant wheel of Cheddar cheese was given to Queen Victoria (1837-1901) for a wedding gift.

How to eat Cheese

  1. Eat cheese from mildest to strongest if it is served on a platter. In general, soft, creamy cheeses are milder than hard ones.
  2. Eat cheese at room temperature to help bring out the flavors and textures.
  3. Peel or cut the rind off of hard cheeses. The rind is usually hard and waxy.
